Berry and Spinach Smoothie

Description

This spinach berry smoothie is an easy way to get kids to eat their fruits and

veggies. If you want a fruitier taste, exclude the yogurt.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups frozen berries
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup fresh spinach, or to taste
  • 5 strawberries

Steps

  1. Blend berries, yogurt, orange juice, spinach, and strawberries together in a blender until smooth.
